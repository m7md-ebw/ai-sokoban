What I Figured Out

  • When you're working with older tools like gym, it's super important to match the right versions of each library.
  • Sometimes the official packages are missing stuff (like rendering), but you can fix it if you know what to look for.
  • You don’t always need to switch tools — you can just adjust things to make them work.
